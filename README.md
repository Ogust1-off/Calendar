# Calendar by Shortcut™

Your ECAM schedule, always with you.

---

## Install on iPhone

1. Open the [app link](https://ogust1-off.github.io/Calendar/) in **Safari** (not Chrome)
2. Tap the **Share** button ⎙ at the bottom
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add** — the app appears on your home screen

The app works offline once installed.

---

## First launch

You'll be asked for two things:

**1. A Google API key**
- Go to [console.cloud.google.com](https://console.cloud.google.com)
- New project → APIs & Services → Credentials → Create API Key
- Restrict it to the **Google Calendar API**

**2. Your Calendar ID(s)**
- Open Google Calendar on desktop
- ⚙️ Settings → your calendar → "Integrate calendar"
- Copy the **Calendar ID**

Your credentials are stored only on your device.

---

## Share

Send the app link to others. Each person sets up their own calendars on first launch.

---

## Settings

Tap **⚙️** in the top-right corner to update your calendars or API key at any time.
